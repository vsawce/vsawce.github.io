About Me

Hello! I am a Computer Engineering student at San Jose State graduating in December 2023. I have a passion within embedded software and hardware, and have designed, validated, and managed numerous projects within embedded systems.

I am currently in my senior design project, and am designing a fully in-house battery management system (BMS) at all levels including the PCB and also the firmware. This BMS will be the foundation for future revisions within Spartan Racing FSAE, part of the FSAE Electric (vehicle) design series.

At Spartan Racing FSAE, I'm also the ex-Software Lead Engineer and now Software Advisor. Within this team, I have over-sought the instrument cluster, CAN-sensors module, electronics control unit, battery management system, and other miscellaneous embedded systems relevant to the car.
