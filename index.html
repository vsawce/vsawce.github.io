Spartan Racing BMS Senior Project

MAR 2023 - DEC 2023+

My senior project is an in-house distributed battery management system (BMS) for a 600V system, custom designed particularly for SJSU's Formula SAE team. As the team was looking for a new BMS solution for future racecar iterations, I found this to be an excellent opportunity to pursue as my senior project and to further my embedded systems development skills.

In a project group of four, I took the lead in driving the direction of this project, having experience and connections with the Spartan Racing team. As of 10/17/2023, a custom BMS master PCB has already been designed, assembled, and tested. We are able to successfully read cell voltages on an old Spartan Racing BMS module over USB. The BMS slave schematic is currently in review and is to undergo layout design.

The distributed BMS master-slave architecture is designed with isoSPI for its master-slave. The BMS master is based on the STM32F105 platform, and the BMS slave is based on the LTC6813 battery stack monitor.
